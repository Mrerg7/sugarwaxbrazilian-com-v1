NATURAL • GENTLE • EFFECTIVE

The premium natural alternative to traditional Brazilian waxing

Sugar wax Brazilian (also known as Brazilian sugaring) is an ancient, natural hair removal method using a paste made of sugar, lemon, and water. Applied at body temperature, it is gentler on the skin than hot wax and has been used for centuries for smooth, long-lasting results with fewer ingrown hairs.

Less Painful & Skin-Friendly

The sugar paste sticks only to hair and dead skin cells — not live skin. Ideal for sensitive areas and clients who find traditional waxing too harsh.

Reduces Ingrown Hairs

Applied against hair growth and removed with the growth direction. This technique minimizes breakage and trauma, leading to smoother skin and fewer ingrowns over time.

Long-Lasting Results

Clients enjoy 4–6 weeks of smooth skin. Regular treatments often result in finer, sparser hair growth. Perfect for clients seeking a natural, low-maintenance routine.

100% Natural & Easy Cleanup

Water-soluble paste rinses away cleanly with warm water — no sticky residue. Free from harsh chemicals, resins, and parabens found in many traditional waxes.
